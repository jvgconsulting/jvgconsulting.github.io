Our Services

Human Resource Management

We provide complete cycle covering from talent acquisition to retirement facility, including skill mapping, skill gaps and development inputs with complete roadmap. Our recruitment techniques are mix of social media hiring (thru Linkdin, Facebook, Twitter etc.), machine-learning / AI tools apart from Head-hunting, Focused Data Mapping etc, apart from the traditional way of hiring thru portals (most effective). Our approach will help you in looking at the overall personality of the candidate apart from his technical and / functional expertise to handle the role. Apart from recruitment we also facilitate in design/map employee communication letters such as increment letters, promotion letters and transfer letters etc for existing employees.

Contractual / Temp Staffing

Our service will relieve you from stress of recruiting skilled resources, managing compensations, increment, promotion, layoff, etc. Given our connects with multiple companies in your industry, we can facilitate you in providing skilled contractual staff at your disposal. This gives you ample of time in focusing on key business areas, keeping your business lean and mean.

Payroll Processing

For a growing organization HR team needs to be flexible in keeping pace with growing business. Given the team size and complexity involved in HR administrative works can be a time-consuming activity. We can help you in not only standardizing the activities by defining a process, but also get it off you back by working as an extended processing unit. We facilitate our customer in Employee record management, Leave Administration, Payslip preparation, payment processing, etc. All these are done with complete confidentiality of individua data, while providing necessary MIS for senior management to take decision.

Statutory Compliance

With our experience team across industries, we are in position to not only interpret applicable rules for each setup, but also provide recommendations to be compliant. We can facilite you in areas like Provident Fund (PF) Management.Employee State Insurance Corporation (ESIC) Management, Professional Tax Management, Income tax calculations & issue of Form – 16, Generation of challans for statutory payments or Contract workers Management.

IR & Legal Services

On Manufacturing side its often said that Industrial Relationship is the key for successful employer-employee relationship. Labor management is an important aspect that needs to be handled delicately as most of the HR team is used to handle discipline nonunion employee base. IR is a complex subject to handle as we need to do a balancing act between semiskilled labor, strong union and stringent labor laws. Our team are experience people on legal aspects of IR cases, who can not only be your advisor but also step in building up a strong case with right inputs.

The key aspect is to build a high performance team that’s well set for larger goals with strong employer-employee relationship.
